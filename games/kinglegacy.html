How to Redeem Roblox King Legacy Codes?

To redeem Roblox King Legacy codes, follow these steps:
  1. Launch King Legacy on Roblox.
  2. Click the "Menu" button located at the bottom of the screen (near your Health bar).
  3. Click the Shop icon (the shopping cart).
  4. Scroll to the very bottom of the Shop menu.
  5. Enter your code in the text box and hit Redeem.
  6. If the code is valid and active, you will receive a confirmation message and the reward will be added to your account.
